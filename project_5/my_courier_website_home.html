Who Are We?

The story of My Courier, began almost 10 years ago, as a family business. How we evolved into a thousand-dollar company reflects the history of modern transportation, international commerce, logistics and financial services. Today, My Courier is customer first, people led, innovation driven. It’s powered by more than 495,000 employees connecting more than 220 nations and territories across roads, rails, air, and ocean. Tomorrow, My Courier will continue to lead the industry and connect the world, with a commitment to quality service and environmental sustainability. This is our purpose. It’s the essence of our DNA – the foundation of our work. It reflects our desire to do what’s right, to serve others, and to improve the quality of life for people around the world. It’s not just what we do-- it’s why we do it. It guides every decision, every day. Simply put, it’s the reason we are here.

Services
Door 2 Door

Place your order online for door-to-door parcel collection and delivery services and have your items picked up and delivered directly to you. Book simple and fast deliveries with My Courier. We offer worldwide door-to-door shipping services with the best rates on the market.

Point 2 Door

My Courier service points serve as drop-off and pick-up points for your parcels. This is history in the making everyone! My Courier, our popular low-cost courier service is now offering a point-to-door feature. This means that your parcel can now go directly to your customer’s doorstep from your drop-off point.

Point 2 Point

The sender delivers his shipment in an My Courier service point, and the recipient receives it from the corresponding My Courier destination service point, enjoying a discount up to 20% (My Courier undertakes the telephone notice of the recipient regarding the arrival of his shipment).

Next Day Delivery (Express)

Next-day delivery has become so popular with buyers and sellers alike, it’s become the new industry standard. Plus now with My Courier, next-day delivery isn't just possible - it’s affordable too. Thanks to our extensive selection of expedited delivery options, we’re confident that you’ll find the right next day delivery option that matches your requirements - as well as your budget – whatever the size of your parcel delivery.
